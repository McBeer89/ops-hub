Where these numbers come from

Counter matrix: ~950 simulation batteries on the community battle engine (nexuslegacytools). Every stat visible on in-game ship cards matches the engine exactly, but shield pools, shield regen, and the round cap are the model's reconstruction — unvalidated against large real battle reports. Pure-type duels at vanilla tech; mixed fleets shift rapid-fire economics substantially. Carrier row excludes wing buffs; bomber ×3 vs defense buildings not shown.

Intel: joined from nexuslegacytools public JSON dumps (players, alliances, per-arm universe data, 7-day histories). Score/ranking snapshots only — no private account data exists in the dumps. The station/planet feed lags live play. Tracked alliances are added as we gather intel on them.
